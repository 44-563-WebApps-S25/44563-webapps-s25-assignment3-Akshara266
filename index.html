Favorite Food: Paneer Butter Masala

I love Paneer Butter Masala because of its rich,creamy texture and the perfect balance of spices. It’s a delicious North Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy, blended with butter and cream.

How to Make Paneer Butter Masala

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Chop tomatoes, onions, garlic, and ginger.
    2. Cut paneer into cubes.
    3. Gather all the spices (turmeric, cumin, garam masala, etc.).
  2. Cook the Base
    1. Heat butter in a pan.
    2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until golden brown.
    3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
    4. Blend the mixture into a smooth paste.
  3. Final Assembly
    1. Heat more butter and add the paste.
    2. Add spices, cream, and paneer cubes.
    3. Simmer for a few minutes and serve hot!

  • Jeera Rice
