Jade Sage and Jason

Jade Sage is the protector of the forests, using the power of nature to control the elements and fight against evil. With an unbreakable bond with plants, Jade Sage can summon vines, create healing herbs, and manipulate the wind.

Jason, the loyal sidekick, provides tactical support and enhanced senses, ensuring Jade Sage never fights alone.

Super Powers

  • Jade Sage: Controls plant life and wind, heals with natural energy.
  • Jade Sage: Camouflages within the environment, becoming invisible in forests.
  • Jason: Has enhanced night vision and tracking abilities.
